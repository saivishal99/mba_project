Instructions to Play

  • Hai, this is a stock simulated game consisting of 4 stocks A B C and D.
  • please open the experiment on google chrome
  • The aim of this game is maximize your returns.
  • The timeframe of the game is just7 minutes.
  • x-axis depicts 28 quarters in a year. "if it is shown as 7 on the x-axis it means 7th quarter".
  • stocks should be purchased after the 4th quarter.
  • The y-axis depicts the price.
  • The trading can start only after the stock reaches point 3 on the x-axis.
  • Initial amount of 10000 is provided to trade.The total cash box depicts the amount of cash available to trade.
  • The number of share to be bought should be entered in the buy box and then click on buy. In the same manner for sell option too.
  • If the buy order is not executed,then the required amount of cash is not available.
  • The game ends when the trend stops.
  • There is a button,save.click on it after the game ends and a document will be saved.
  • happy playing.click play
